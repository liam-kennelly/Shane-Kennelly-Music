FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I send my songs for Mixing?

Usually I will start a song with you from scratch, but if you want to send me a song to mix I need all the stems fully dry, with no effects, exported to me in a .Wav format.

2. How do I send my songs for Mastering?

Usually I master the clients I already mix for so I should have the audio files already, but if I don’t just send the stems already mixed and I will master and enhance the audio of your song.

3. When will I get my songs back?

This all depends on the type of song you record. If you come in to my studio you will always get a draft mp3 of what’s been done. Depending on how you record and how prepared you are as an artist, we could have your song finished in one session.

4. Are my songs radio ready after mixing?

Great question! So I always ensure that you get the best mix but it’s the mastering that gets your song ready for the radio. I offer very good prices that will have your song ready and sounding like your already a mainstream artist!

5. Would you happy to collaborate on a project?

If you would like to discuss collaborations please contact me through the contact form or email me: Shanekennelly18@yahoo.com

6. Do you have a recording studio on site?

Yes! In fact I do, and I have a high end Antelope Interface , Adams monitors and a Rode NT1 microphone. I also work at an amazing studio in Scarborough called foreign wav which I can mix you at as well depending on your location

7. Do you have COVID-19 Safety Protocols in place?

Yes! Social distancing is strongly enforced on site and hand washing facilities are available. Everyone (Including Me) is required to wear a mask unless in the recording booth alone.
